FAQ

Who developed this service?
Alan Pope and Stuart Langridge with some help from Daniel McGuire.
What happens with a click package I upload?
The click package is added to a queue to be processed. A bot will pick up the click and install it on a device of your choosing. The application is run and screenshots are taken. The screenshots and log files are emailed to you soon after.
How long does it take to run the app
Under best-case scenario a few minutes for the app to install, run and the email to arrive. If there's a long queue then you may have to wait a while.
What devices can apps be tested on?
Currently the LG Nexus 4 and Asus Nexus 7 (2013) devices are supported. More devices will be made available shortly.
What limits are there?
Currently applications uploaded will have the following restrictions:-
  • Apps must pass the same click review tools as used in the Ubuntu App Store
  • Devices have no gps, wifi or cellular access. We're evaluating how to provide this and may do in the future.
  • Webapps are rejected as they will require network access, which isn't available
  • Scopes are allowed, but their functionality may be limited given the lack of network access
